# Deadhand Mascot — Hank, the night-watchman

The face of the brand. Read alongside [brand-voice.md](brand-voice.md) — Hank
*is* the two-mode voice made visible.

## Who he is

**Hank** — a loyal skeleton on the night shift who keeps his hand on the dead
man's switch so you can sleep. The name is the wink: dead-**HAND** → Hank, a
dependable guy you'd trust with the keys.

He does **not** bring death — he stands guard against it. So he is a watchman,
never a reaper: **no scythe, no hood, no graveyard.** Those belong to the thing
he's protecting you from.

North star: **Manny Calavera (Grim Fandango)** — death made warm and dapper,
film-noir lines, a calaca with a smirk. Día de los Muertos in spirit (death
made playful), but the palette stays ours: amber on charcoal, not carnival neon.

## The two modes (the whole character)

Hank has two gears, and the contrast *is* the brand. Always design him able to
show both — side by side is the strongest single image we own.

| | At rest — everything alive | Something died |
|---|---|---|
| **Pose** | Leaned back, boots up on the console, thermos in hand | Bolt upright, one bony finger pointing / slamming the plunger |
| **Sockets** | Half-lidded, faint smirk | Wide, zero smile |
| **Light** | Green "on watch" LED, amber glow | Red, hard |
| **Line** | "All quiet. Sleep well." | "nightly-backup. 47 min. Go look." |

Recovery beat: a single bony thumbs-up — *"It lives."*

## Anatomy & wardrobe

- **Skull:** large, readable, rounded — not anatomical horror. Eye-sockets are
  expressive dot-pupils that emote. Default: a faint chipped smirk.
- **Bones:** thick, rounded, cartoon calaca — not a medical skeleton.
- **Hero prop:** the **dead man's switch plunger from the logo.** His hand
  rests on it always — this ties mascot and mark into one system.
- **Night-shift kit (this is what makes him warm, not grim):** a knit beanie,
  a wrapped scarf (it's cold on the night shift), and a **thermos of coffee** —
  he never sleeps, the coffee is the running joke.
- **Día de los Muertos, dosed:** faint marigold etching on the skull, amber as
  the marigold note. Never blow out the palette.

## Palette (strict — per brand color semantics)

- Background — charcoal `#0a0b0d`
- Bone — warm cream `#e7e9ec`
- Linework / glow — amber `#f5a524` (alive, dominant → warmth)
- "On watch" LED — green `#46b06f`
- Red `#e5484d` — **alert mode only**

## Where Hank lives

The nav logo stays the vector plunger mark. Hank shows up in: empty states,
404, the waitlist-confirm screen, social avatar, OG image, onboarding,
alert/recovery illustrations, loaders.

## Hard don'ts

No scythe, no reaper hood, no tombstones, no cobwebs, no blood/gore, no
menacing bared-teeth snarl, no spooky-horror vibe. Don't drift the palette into
carnival neon. Don't let red dominate at rest — amber carries the warmth.
